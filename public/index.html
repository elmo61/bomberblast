    How to Play

    Aim: Be the last player standing! Blow up the wooden crates and trap your friends in your bomb blasts — but don't get caught in your own explosion.

    Controls

    • D-pad / WASD / Arrows Move one tile at a time
    • BOMB / Space Drop a bomb

    Power-ups (drop from smashed crates)

    • Extra Bomb — place one more bomb at a time
    • Bigger Blast — your explosions reach further
    • Speed Up — move faster
